# General Keybindings
- Cursor Up                     | Alt-k or Up
- Cursor Down                   | Alt-j or Down
- Select                        | Alt-l or Right or Enter
- Abort                         | Alt-h or Left or Ctrl-c or ESC
- Multi-Select (GitAddSelector) | Tab or Shift-Tab
- Preview-Up                    | Ctrl-k
- Preview-Down                  | Ctrl-j
- Toggle-Preview                | Alt-i
